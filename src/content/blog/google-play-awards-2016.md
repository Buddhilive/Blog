Google have decided to the best Android apps and games we will give Google Play Awards for the first time.

  

Five nominees from a total of ten categories are selected from Google Play Store. The nominees were selected by an expert panel of the Google Play team considering the quality, degree of innovation of the app and having a launch or major update in the last 12 months. The winners in each category will be announced at the Google I / O in May.

  

Here is the complete list of categories and nominees:
